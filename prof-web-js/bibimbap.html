Bibimbap


Ingredients

  • Leftover veggies
  • Cooked Egg
  • Gochujang paste
  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • Honey
  • Sesame oil
  • Garlic
  • Cooked rice



    • Directions

    1. In a non-stick pan add sesame oil, garlic, pepper, salt and all the veggies. Cook in medium high heat until soft-ish

    2. To make the gochujang paste, in a seperate bowl add gochujang, sesame oil, honey and mix

    3. In a bowl serve with rice, cooked veggies, gochujang sauce and top it with the egg
