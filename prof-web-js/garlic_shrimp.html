Garlic Butter Shrimp


Ingredients

  • Cleaned shrimp, with removed shells
  • Garlic diced
  • Butter
  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • Kashmiri red chilli powder
  • Cumin powder
  • Coriander Powder
  • Briyani masala/powder



    • Directions

    1. In a non-stick pan add butter and the garlic, cook until aromatic and a little brown

    2. Add the shrimp and all the spices and cook for 5-ish minutes

    3. Add a tiny bit of water, and cook until most of the water has been evaporated, leaving a film on the shrimp

    4. Serve with carbs / veggies
