Shakshuka


Ingredients

  • Leftover tomato pasta sauce
  • Egg
  • Cayenne pepper
  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • Coriandar powder
  • Cumin powder
  • Paprika
  • Feta/cream cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Onion



    • Directions

    1. In a pan add the oil, diced onions and cook until soft

    2. Next add your leftover pasta sauce, all the spices and let it simmer for 6-7 minutes in medium high heat

    3. Add water if too much sauce has evaporated

    4. Crack some eggs and place it gently on the top of the sauce. Cover with lid and cook until eggs slightly runny

    5. Serve with butterd toast / Garlic bread / Naan and garnish with cheese
