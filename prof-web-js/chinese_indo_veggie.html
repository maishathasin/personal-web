Hakka Chinese / Indo Vegetables


Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Ginger & Garlic paste
  • Ginger & Garlic shredded
  • Oil
  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Raw papaya
  • Carrot
  • Bell peppers
  • Onion
  • Soy sauce
  • Corn starch



    • Directions

    1. In a bowl add chicken breast, garlic/ginger paste

    2. On a non-stick pan add oil and add garlic, cook on medium-high for 5-ish minutes

    3. Add the beef and cook until brown (Optional: add veggies like bell peppers, brocolli to make it healthier)

    4. Add a little bit of soy sauce, the beef should not be drowning in soy sauce

    5. After the soy sauce has been reduced and looks thicker, add a little bit of sugar. And cook until reduced again

    6. Serve with rice or any other carb
